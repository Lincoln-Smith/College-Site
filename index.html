Rowan University!

Rowan University is known for its strong programs in engineering, business, and education. It has a growing reputation for innovation and offers a wi de range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines.

General Information:


Location

Rowan is located in Glassboro, New Jersey, inside of Gloucester County

Established In

The university was founded on September 24, 1923

Mascot

The mascot of Rowan University is Whoo RU, also known as the Prof

Academic Programs:


Good for Doctors!

1 of only 4 universities in the nation to grant both M.D. and D.O. medical degrees

Honor Societies

Rowan's honor societies include: Alpha Epsilon Lambda, Alpha Alpha Alpha, Lambda Alpha, and Phi Alpha Theta

Digging into Programs

80 bachelor's and 60 master's degree programs, five doctoral programs and two professional programs

Student Life:


Student Body Stats

Rowan hosts a total of 19,535 students with 15,231 being undergrads

Retention

85% students return sophomore year

Full-Time Undergrads

87% of students seek a degree with a full-time course load
